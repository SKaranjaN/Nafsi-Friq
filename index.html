about


Josphat Mwangi is a visionary entrepreneur and the founder of Nafsi Friq, a leading organization in the events planning, events sponsoring, and digital marketing space. With over 10 years of experience in the events industry, Josphat has established himself as a thought leader and innovator in the field.

Josphat founded Nafsi Friq with a mission to transform the events industry in Europe and across Africa. Through his leadership, Nafsi Friq has grown into a successful organization, with a reputation for delivering exceptional events and innovative digital marketing solutions.

Today, Nafsi Friq works with a wide range of clients, from small businesses to multinational corporations, providing tailored event planning and digital marketing services to meet their unique needs. Josphat's dedication to excellence and commitment to innovation have earned him recognition as one of the most influential entrepreneurs in Africa and Europe.
